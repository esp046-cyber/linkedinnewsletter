🔧 Deploy Your Free Proxy
What & Why
Browsers block direct calls to Notion, Airtable, Mailchimp, Clearbit, SendGrid, and others due to CORS security policy. A Cloudflare Worker acts as a tiny forwarder — it receives requests from this app and forwards them to those APIs with your keys. It's free forever (100,000 requests/day on Cloudflare's free plan) and takes under 2 minutes to deploy.
Your keys never leave your proxy. The proxy runs under your own Cloudflare account. ACRBS only sends the target API name and payload — your stored secrets are injected server-side.
Step 1 — Create Cloudflare Account
Go to dash.cloudflare.com/sign-up and create a free account if you don't have one. No credit card required.
Step 2 — Create a Worker
In your Cloudflare dashboard: Workers & Pages → Create → Create Worker. Name it acrbs-proxy. Click "Deploy".
Step 3 — Paste Worker Code
Click "Edit code" on your Worker. Replace all existing code with this. This is the complete production proxy:
After pasting, click Deploy.
Step 4 — Add Your Secrets
In your Worker: Settings → Variables → Add variable. Add these as Secret (encrypted) variables for each service you use:
NOTION_TOKEN = secret_… AIRTABLE_TOKEN = pat… MAILCHIMP_KEY = …-us1 AC_KEY = your_activecampaign_key AC_URL = https://youracccount.api-us1.com CLEARBIT_KEY = sk_… HUNTER_KEY = your_hunter_key SENDGRID_KEY = SG.… SENDGRID_FROM = you@yourdomain.com WA_TOKEN = EAA… WA_PHONE_ID = your_phone_number_id SF_TOKEN = your_salesforce_token SF_URL = https://yourorg.my.salesforce.com
Only add the ones you need. All others can be left empty.
Step 5 — Paste Your Proxy URL
Your Worker URL looks like: https://acrbs-proxy.YOUR-SUBDOMAIN.workers.dev
